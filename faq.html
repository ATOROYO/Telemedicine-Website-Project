Frequently Asked Questions

What is telemedicine? +

Telemedicine is the practice of using technology to provide medical care remotely. It allows patients to consult with healthcare professionals through video calls, phone calls, or messaging platforms.

How do I book an appointment? +

You can book an appointment through our 'Consultation' page. Select the specialty and preferred doctor, choose a suitable date and time, and provide your contact details.

Is my information secure? +

Yes, your information is secure. We use advanced encryption and security protocols to ensure the confidentiality and safety of your personal and medical data.
